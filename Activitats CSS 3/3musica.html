The Retourn of Headhunterz

Género: Electronic
Estilo: Hardstyle
Año: 2018

Headhunterz released his third studio album titled The Return of Headhunterz on 2 March 2018, which was dubbed as his "return to hardstyle production" by Dancing Astronaut.

On 2 February 2018 Headhunterz announced the inception of his new label in collaboration with Wildstylez, Art of Creation. The label is described as "a dynamic platform where years of experience, friendship, talent and passion come together and spark new inspiration on the pursuit for quality and greatness in music." As well as he has described that Sub Zero Project have some of the “Best Hardstyle Productions since his own”
